# Analysis of Higher Education Statistics in Uzbekistan

## Project Overview

This project provides a comprehensive data-driven analysis of the higher education landscape in Uzbekistan. By leveraging national datasets from **stat.edu.uz**, the study identifies key trends in student demographics, institutional preferences, popular academic fields, and the growing internationalization of Uzbek universities.

##Key Findings

Based on the analysis of the most recent national datasets, the following insights were identified:

* **Regional Distribution:** The majority of the student population originates from the **Qashqadaryo** and **Samarqand** regions, as well as **Tashkent City**.
* **Top Institutions:** The three universities with the highest student enrollment are:
1. Tashkent State University of Economics (**TSUE**)
2. International School of Finance and Technology (**ISFT**)
3. Tashkent International University of Chemistry (**Toshkent xalqaro kimyo universiteti**)


* **Institutional Shift:** **56%** of students are currently enrolled in **state universities**, while **44%** have opted for private or international institutions, reflecting a rapidly diversifying educational market.
* **Popular Majors:** The top three academic fields chosen by students are:

* **Internationalization:** Uzbekistan currently hosts **17,597 international students**. The top three countries of origin for these students are **India**, **Turkmenistan**, and **Pakistan**.

##Methodology

1. 
**Data Acquisition:** Extracted raw data from the State Committee of the Republic of Uzbekistan on Statistics.


2. 
**Processing:** Cleaned and formatted data in Excel to ensure consistency in regional naming conventions.


3. 
**Analysis:** Performed deep-dive SQL queries to identify correlations between regional origins and major selection.


4. 
**Visualization:** Developed a Power BI dashboard to present these findings in an accessible format for stakeholders.
